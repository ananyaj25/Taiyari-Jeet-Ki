Sitting back,recalling old memories and living in the comforts of the past time is a pleasure we all enjoy. “Yaadon Ki गुल्लक” evokes sentimental yearning or desire for the return of some past period. These beautiful,free spirited times could be revisited by remembering old movies, shows, cartoons,songs their references along with some exciting quizzes, which our website sought to do using hyperlinks, adding a jukebox to our website, and will contain some fun facts about your favorite characters. You would be taken back to the times, when big CDs were popular instead of tiny PDs, or DDLJ cassettes were played on the walkmans.The golden period of the 90s and the early 2000s. Come dive into the world of innocence, free of the social media fish market.A place of comfort. A place which feels like home.

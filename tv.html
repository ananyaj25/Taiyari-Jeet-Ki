And hence our website should dedicate one space to our beloved TV Shows too! We grew up watching so amazing shows, which went down in the history as masterpieces, and no-one can ever match up to them. We grew up in an era when television as a whole was growing, and we have witnessed many firsts and they turned out to be all time favourites. Ramayan



Mera Namaskar sweekar kijiye priye jano. Give yourself a cheer, if you all started talking in “Shuddh Hindi” after watching the most viewed TV Show of all time, Ramayan. Being an epic, it not just gave us values but also unified us in many ways. No matter how many times it was re-telecasted, we all watched it with same enthusiasm again and again. Probably we still remember its amazingly composed few of the bhajans and songs, like “Hum Katha sunate Raam sakal gundhaam ki”.






,



Tarak Mehta ka Ulta chashma




Taarak Mehta Ka Ooltah Chashma- Started way back in the year 2008, TMKOC is no doubt one of the most watched and loved show for not just the present generation but also the past one. Showing us the true colours of “Unity in Diversity”, TMKOC has taught us a lot of values, and all the members of Gokuldhaam society are so relatable that we can connect them with any of our relatives and neighbours. They way they celebrate festivals of all religions and caste, how they stay together, and help each other, TMKOC always generates a spark that we all can live peacefully yet following our own traditions with full zeal!





Mahabharat





BR Chopra’s Mahabharat is yet another example of how a successful show looks like. I am sure that everyone still remembers the epic opening music composition of the show, which ends with a Geeta Shlok. Whether it was shakuni mama’s game plan, or Bhishma Pita Mah lying on his deathbed, we all felt each and every emotion real hard, because of the so amazing star cast. I still imagine Krishna ji to look the same way as that of the Mahabharat one!
